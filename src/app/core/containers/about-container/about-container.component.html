About Us
Who Are We? Bluey Shop is an art company based out of Louisville, Kentucky. Created in September of 2020, we initially aimed to sell our work locally to friends and family, but with the help of our website-making wizard, Steven, our art can reach you all across the nation!
What Do We Do? We paint skateboards. Every board is completely unique and hand poured by one of us. We use acrylic paints and various pouring techniques for an abstract, mesmerising design and apply a clear coat for a shiny finish. Our designs don’t always turn out exactly as planned, so we learned to let the paint guide the way!
Art or Skateboard? Steven and Andrew share a passion for skateboarding that has led to years of experience for both of them. After spending many hours riding these boards, we can confirm that they are good quality and durable to skate. They are made of 7-ply, 100% Canadian Maple wood. It’s up to you whether or not your board should be admired on the wall or the pavement.
